Return Policy





Return Policy for all items purchased from T-ShirtCo.


  1. The customer must inform us of returning an item within 2 days after the item has been received. In addition, the customer must notify us of the date that the item has been recieved.

  2. The customer will receive 100% money back guaranteed if they are not satisfied with the product or the customer can exchange to another similar product.

  3. The cost for shipping will be paid by us as part of our refund, however, the customer must pay for the shipping to return the item.

  4. The item purchased by the customer must be in its original condition and all tags have not been removed.
